Dreamer

Dream to Control: Learning Behaviors by Latent Imagination — Hafner et al., ICLR 2020

🔗 danijar.com/dreamer
💡
Core Ideas
🌍

World Model

Learn a latent dynamics model from experience to predict future states, rewards, and observations in a compact state space

💭

Latent Imagination

Imagine thousands of trajectories entirely in the learned latent space — no environment interaction needed during behavior learning

📈

Actor-Critic in Imagination

Learn an actor and value function by backpropagating analytic gradients of multi-step returns through the learned dynamics

🧩
World Model Components (RSSM)

Representation Model

\( p(s_t \mid s_{t-1}, a_{t-1}, o_t) \)

Encodes observations and past states into compact latent states using the RSSM + CNN encoder

Transition Model

\( q(s_t \mid s_{t-1}, a_{t-1}) \)

Predicts next latent state without seeing the observation — enables imagination without rendering

Reward Model

\( q(r_t \mid s_t) \)

Predicts scalar rewards from latent states to evaluate imagined trajectories

Observation Model (for learning)

\( q(o_t \mid s_t) \)

Reconstructs images from latent states — provides learning signal via pixel reconstruction loss

Training Objectives

Dynamics Learning: ELBO Objective

\( \mathcal{J}_{\text{REC}} = \mathbb{E}_p \left[ \sum_t \ln q(o_t | s_t) + \ln q(r_t | s_t) - \beta \, \text{KL}\left[ p(s_t | s_{t-1}, a_{t-1}, o_t) \,\|\, q(s_t | s_{t-1}, a_{t-1}) \right] \right] \)

Reconstruction of observations + rewards, regularized by KL divergence between posterior and prior (encourages consistent latent dynamics)

Actor Objective: Maximize Imagined Returns

\( \max_\phi \; \mathbb{E}_{q_\theta, q_\phi} \left[ \sum_{\tau=t}^{t+H} V_\lambda(s_\tau) \right] \)

Maximize value estimates along imagined trajectories — gradients flow back through the dynamics via reparameterization

Critic Objective: Regress V_λ Targets

\( \min_\psi \; \mathbb{E}_{q_\theta, q_\phi} \left[ \sum_{\tau=t}^{t+H} \frac{1}{2} \| v_\psi(s_\tau) - V_\lambda(s_\tau) \|^2 \right] \)

The value model learns to predict the λ-weighted returns — targets are computed from imagined trajectories (gradient stopped)

Paper Lineage

Builds On

PlaNet
Hafner et al., 2018 — Same RSSM world model, but uses online planning (CEM) instead of learned behaviors. Dreamer replaces planning with actor-critic.
World Models
Ha & Schmidhuber, 2018 — Two-stage world model (VAE + MDN-RNN) with evolved controllers in imagination. Pioneer of learning in latent space.
SAC
Haarnoja et al., 2018 — The tanh-Gaussian policy parameterization and reparameterization trick used in Dreamer's action model.
Dyna
Sutton, 1991 — The classic architecture of learning, planning, and acting with a learned model. Dreamer is a deep learning instantiation of Dyna.

Built Upon By

DreamerV2
Hafner et al., 2021 — Discrete latent states (categorical), KL balancing, achieves human-level Atari from pixels with a single GPU.
DreamerV3
Hafner et al., 2023 — Fixed hyperparameters across domains, symlog predictions, scales from Atari to Minecraft to DMLab without tuning.
DayDreamer
Wu et al., 2022 — Applies Dreamer to real robots (A1 quadruped, UR5 arm), learning locomotion in ~1 hour of real interaction.
TD-MPC / TD-MPC2
Hansen et al., 2022/2024 — Combines learned latent dynamics with model-predictive control and TD-learning. Alternative to pure imagination-based approach.
