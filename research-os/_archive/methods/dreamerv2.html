DreamerV2

Mastering Atari with Discrete World Models — Hafner, Lillicrap, Norouzi, Ba · ICLR 2021

🔗 danijar.com/dreamerv2
💡
Core Ideas
🎲

Discrete Latent Representations

Replace Gaussian latents with 32 categorical variables × 32 classes, creating a sparse binary vector of 1024 with 32 active bits

⚖️

KL Balancing

Asymmetric KL loss with α=0.8 trains the prior faster than it regularizes the posterior, preventing collapse

🔀

Mixed Actor Gradients

Combine Reinforce (unbiased, high variance) with straight-through dynamics backprop (biased, low variance) via mixing ratio ρ

🧩
World Model Components (RSSM)

Recurrent Model

\( h_t = f_\phi(h_{t-1}, z_{t-1}, a_{t-1}) \)

Deterministic GRU backbone that integrates past information into a recurrent state

Representation Model

\( z_t \sim q_\phi(z_t \mid h_t, x_t) \quad \text{[32 categoricals} \times \text{32 classes]} \)

Encodes observations into discrete latent states — 32 categorical variables each with 32 classes

Transition Predictor

\( \hat{z}_t \sim p_\phi(\hat{z}_t \mid h_t) \quad \text{[predicts without image]} \)

Predicts next discrete latent state without seeing the observation — enables imagination

Image Predictor

\( \hat{x}_t \sim p_\phi(\hat{x}_t \mid h_t, z_t) \)

Reconstructs images from the combined deterministic and stochastic state

Reward Predictor

\( \hat{r}_t \sim p_\phi(\hat{r}_t \mid h_t, z_t) \)

Predicts scalar rewards from latent states to evaluate imagined trajectories

Discount Predictor

\( \hat{\gamma}_t \sim p_\phi(\hat{\gamma}_t \mid h_t, z_t) \)

Predicts episode continuation probability — replaces fixed discount with a learned signal

Training Objectives

World Model: ELBO / Variational Free Energy

\( \mathcal{L}(\phi) = \mathbb{E}\left[\sum_t -\ln p(x_t|h_t,z_t) - \ln p(r_t|h_t,z_t) - \ln p(\gamma_t|h_t,z_t) + \beta \cdot \text{KL}\left[q(z_t|h_t,x_t) \| p(z_t|h_t)\right]\right] \)

β=0.1 for Atari, β=1.0 for continuous control. KL uses balancing with α=0.8.

Actor Loss (combined)

\( \mathcal{L}(\psi) = \mathbb{E}\left[\sum_t -\rho \ln p(a_t|s_t) \cdot \text{sg}(V^\lambda_t - v(s_t)) - (1-\rho) \cdot V^\lambda_t - \eta \cdot H[a_t|s_t]\right] \)

For Atari (discrete): ρ=1 (pure REINFORCE). For continuous control: ρ=0 (dynamics backprop). Entropy coefficient η encourages exploration.

Critic Loss

\( \mathcal{L}(\xi) = \mathbb{E}\left[\sum_t \frac{1}{2}\left(v_\xi(s_t) - \text{sg}(V^\lambda_t)\right)^2\right] \)

The critic regresses to the λ-return targets with stop-gradient — standard value function regression.

Paper Lineage

Builds On

Dreamer (V1)
Hafner et al., 2019 — Same RSSM architecture but with Gaussian latents and pure dynamics backprop for actor. V2 switches to discrete latents and mixed gradients.
PlaNet
Hafner et al., 2018 — The original RSSM world model, using online CEM planning instead of learned behaviors.
Straight-Through Estimator
Bengio et al., 2013 — Enables gradient flow through discrete sampling by using softmax probabilities in the backward pass.

Built Upon By

DreamerV3
Hafner et al., 2023 — Adds symlog predictions, free bits, return normalization. Fixed hyperparameters across 150+ tasks in 8 domains.
DayDreamer
Wu et al., 2022 — Applies DreamerV2 to real robots, learning locomotion in ~1 hour.
IRIS
Micheli et al., 2023 — Transformer-based world model with discrete tokens, inspired by DreamerV2's discrete latent approach.
