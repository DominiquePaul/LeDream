World Models

Recurrent World Models Facilitate Policy Evolution — Ha & Schmidhuber, 2018

🔗 worldmodels.github.io
💡
Core Ideas
👁️

Vision-Memory-Controller (V-M-C)

Decompose the agent into V (VAE compresses frames to z), M (MDN-RNN predicts dynamics), and C (tiny linear controller)

💭

Dream Training

Train the controller entirely in hallucinated environments generated by V+M — no real environment interaction during policy optimization

🧬

CMA-ES Optimization

Evolve the controller's 867 parameters using Covariance Matrix Adaptation Evolution Strategy — no gradient-based RL for the policy

🧩
Model Components (V-M-C)

V Model (VAE)

\( z \sim \text{Encoder}(x) \)

Convolutional encoder/decoder compresses 64×64 frames to latent z (32-dim for CarRacing, 64-dim for VizDoom)

M Model (MDN-RNN)

\( P(z_{t+1}) = \sum_{i} \pi_i \, \mathcal{N}(\mu_i, \sigma_i) \)

LSTM-256 + Mixture Density Network output. Predicts P(zt+1 | at, zt, ht) as mixture of K Gaussians. Temperature τ controls sampling.

C Model (Controller)

\( a_t = W_c [z_t ; h_t] + b_c \)

Single linear layer. Only 867 parameters (CarRacing). Optimized by CMA-ES, not gradients.

Dream Environment

\( z_{t+1} \sim \text{MDN-RNN}(a_t, z_t, h_t; \tau) \)

M replaces the real environment. Generates zt+1 by sampling from MDN. τ>1 makes dreams harder/more stochastic.

Training Objectives

VAE Loss (V Model)

$$ \mathcal{L}_{\text{VAE}} = -\mathbb{E}_{q(z|x)}[\log p(x|z)] + \text{KL}[q(z|x) \,\|\, p(z)] $$

Reconstruction loss (pixel-level) plus KL divergence regularization to shape the latent space. Trained independently on collected frames.

MDN-RNN Loss (M Model)

$$ P(z_{t+1} | a_t, z_t, h_t) = \sum_i \pi_i \, \mathcal{N}(z; \mu_i, \sigma_i), \quad \mathcal{L}_{\text{MDN}} = -\log P(z_{t+1}) $$

Negative log-likelihood of the next latent vector under the mixture of Gaussians predicted by the MDN. Trained on sequences of (z, a) pairs.

Controller Objective (CMA-ES)

$$ \max \; \mathbb{E}\left[\sum_t r_t\right] \quad \text{where} \quad a_t = W_c [z_t ; h_t] + b_c $$

Maximize cumulative reward by evolving the weight matrix Wc and bias bc. CMA-ES maintains a distribution over parameter vectors and updates the covariance matrix based on fitness evaluations.

Paper Lineage

Builds On

VAE
Kingma & Welling, 2014 — Variational Autoencoder provides the V model for compressing observations into a continuous latent space.
MDN
Bishop, 1994 — Mixture Density Networks output a mixture of Gaussians, enabling multi-modal predictions in the M model.
RNN Sequence Models
Graves, 2013 — Recurrent neural networks for sequence generation. The LSTM backbone used in the MDN-RNN memory module.
CMA-ES
Hansen, 2001 — Covariance Matrix Adaptation Evolution Strategy replaces gradient-based RL for optimizing the small controller.
Learning to Think
Schmidhuber, 1990–2015 — Early ideas on learning world models and using them for planning and dreaming in recurrent controllers.

Built Upon By

PlaNet
Hafner et al., 2019 — Replaces VAE+MDN-RNN with RSSM and uses online planning (CEM) instead of evolution. First model-based method competitive on continuous control.
Dreamer
Hafner et al., 2020 — Builds on PlaNet with an actor-critic in latent imagination, backpropagating gradients through learned dynamics.
GameGAN
Kim et al., 2020 — Learns a generative model that can simulate entire game environments (Pac-Man) without a game engine, inspired by dream training.
Dreaming Approaches in RL
Various, 2019–present — The idea of training policies in hallucinated environments has become a core paradigm in model-based RL research.
