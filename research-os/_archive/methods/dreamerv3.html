DreamerV3

Mastering Diverse Domains through World Models — Hafner, Pasukonis, Ba, Lillicrap · 2023

🔗 danijar.com/dreamerv3
💡
Core Ideas
📊

Symlog Predictions

Transform targets with symlog(x) = sign(x)·ln(|x|+1) to handle vastly different reward scales without domain-specific tuning

🔧

Fixed Hyperparameters

Single configuration works across 150+ tasks in 8 diverse domains — no tuning needed for new problems

📐

Return Normalization & Free Bits

Percentile-based return normalization (5th-95th) for scale-invariant actor loss; free bits clip KL below 1 nat to prevent collapse

🧩
World Model Components (RSSM V3)

Sequence Model

\( h_t = f_\phi(h_{t-1}, z_{t-1}, a_{t-1}) \)

Deterministic recurrent backbone (GRU) that maintains temporal context across steps

Encoder

\( z_t \sim q_\phi(z_t \mid h_t, x_t) \)

Posterior over discrete categorical latents given recurrent state and observation

Dynamics Predictor

\( \hat{z}_t \sim p_\phi(\hat{z}_t \mid h_t) \)

Prior that predicts latent state without observation — enables imagination

Reward Predictor

\( \hat{r}_t \sim p_\phi(\hat{r}_t \mid h_t, z_t) \)   [symlog squared loss]

Predicts rewards in symlog space for scale-invariant learning across domains

Continue Predictor

\( \hat{c}_t \sim p_\phi(\hat{c}_t \mid h_t, z_t) \)   [Bernoulli]

Predicts episode continuation probability for proper discounting

Decoder

\( \hat{x}_t \sim p_\phi(\hat{x}_t \mid h_t, z_t) \)   [symlog squared loss]

Reconstructs observations in symlog space — provides learning signal for the latent space

Training Objectives

World Model (Three Separate Losses)

\( \mathcal{L}(\phi) = \mathbb{E}\left[\sum_t \beta_{\text{pred}} \cdot \mathcal{L}_{\text{pred}} + \beta_{\text{dyn}} \cdot \mathcal{L}_{\text{dyn}} + \beta_{\text{rep}} \cdot \mathcal{L}_{\text{rep}}\right] \)
\( \mathcal{L}_{\text{pred}} = -\ln p(x_t | z_t, h_t) - \ln p(r_t | z_t, h_t) - \ln p(c_t | z_t, h_t) \)
\( \mathcal{L}_{\text{dyn}} = \max\!\big(1,\; \text{KL}[\,\text{sg}(q(z_t|h_t,x_t)) \,\|\, p(z_t|h_t)\,]\big) \)   ← free bits
\( \mathcal{L}_{\text{rep}} = \max\!\big(1,\; \text{KL}[\,q(z_t|h_t,x_t) \,\|\, \text{sg}(p(z_t|h_t))\,]\big) \)   ← free bits

βpred=1, βdyn=1, βrep=0.1. Free bits (max with 1 nat) prevent KL from collapsing while focusing capacity on prediction loss.

Critic (Distributional with Twohot)

\( \mathcal{L}(\psi) = -\sum_t \ln\, p_\psi\!\left(R^\lambda_t \,\big|\, s_t\right) \)

Critic outputs a softmax distribution over exponentially-spaced symexp bins. Twohot encoding places probability mass on two adjacent bins. This decouples gradient scale from target magnitude.

Actor (REINFORCE with Return Normalization)

\( \mathcal{L}(\theta) = -\sum_t \text{sg}\!\left(\frac{R^\lambda_t - v_\psi(s_t)}{\max(1, S)}\right) \cdot \ln \pi_\theta(a_t | s_t) + \eta \cdot H[\pi_\theta(a_t | s_t)] \)
\( S = \text{EMA}\!\left(\text{Per}(R^\lambda, 95) - \text{Per}(R^\lambda, 5),\; 0.99\right) \)

Advantages normalized by 5th-95th percentile range with floor max(1, S) to avoid amplifying noise. Entropy bonus η encourages exploration.
